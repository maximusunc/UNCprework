Max Wang, Tubist

Bio

Max is a freelance tubist in the Triangle area, playing in a plethora of orchestras across the state. His main obligation is with the Durham Symphony, which he has held the principal tuba position for 5 seasons. He has a bachelor's degree in music education from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and a master's degree in music performance from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. Currently, he works for the Tuba Exchange in Durham, NC as a Artist in Residence where he gets to play a bunch of different tubas!

Orchestras

  • Durham Symphony, Principal Tuba
  • North Carolina Symphony, Tuba Sub
  • Wilmington Symphony, Tuba Sub
  • Tar River Orchestra, Tuba Sub
  • Chamber Orchestra of the Triangle, Tuba Sub
